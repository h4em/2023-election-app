2023 election app

About

This app allows you to explore detailed distribution of votes from the 2023 Polish Parliamentary election across various locations. Made with the official governmental data available @wybory.gov.pl/sejmsenat2023. If you wish to learn more visit the project's repo. Use the searchbar to lookup a place :).

Notes

Some things worth mentioning:

  • Data from abroad / ships was excluded.
  • Consider opting for broader categories such as 'City' and above in the searchbar dropdown for most reliable results.
  • The 'Address' category works for different formats, like place names (e.g., 'Szkola Podstawowa nr. xxx'), streets, cities, and post codes.
