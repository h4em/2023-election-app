About

This is a solo CS student project I made on the side that let's you check the exact votes distribution of the 2023 Polish Parliamentary election. Made from the official governmental data available at wybory.gov.pl/sejmsenat2023. I can't guarantee the results are 100% correct, but from the tests i ran (you can check them out at the project's repo) i believe they're somewhat accurate.

Notes

Some things worth mentioning:

  • Lookup for category 'Address' can sometimes produce unreliable results due to the original data source format. Choosing more broader range is recommended (categories 'City' and above).
  • 'Address' category can take both the place name e.g. 'Szkola Podstawowa nr. xxx' as well as street, city names and post codes.
  • Data from abroad/ships was excluded.
